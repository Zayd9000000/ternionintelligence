Team

Felix founded Hartmann Capital in 2018 at the age of 23, launching one of the earliest crypto hedge funds directly from his bedroom. Starting with around $200k, he scaled Hartmann Capital into a ~$50 million frontier tech investment firm. Initially making his mark as a multi-strategy digital asset manager, Felix repeatedly achieved top-3 industry rankings between 2020 and 2023 (Source: Galaxy VisionTrack). Since 2022, he has expanded into early-stage venture investing, positioning Hartmann Capital in the top decile of its vintage cohort by IRR, TVPI, and DPI (Source: Carta VC Fund Performance Report). Felix also serves strategically on the boards of ShapesXR, TrippVR, Squido, Sinn, and Cathedral Studios. Beyond investments, Felix actively explores longevity science and writes dystopian fiction.

Felix Hartmann

Managing Partner & CIO

Daniel joined Hartmann Capital in 2021, specializing in the intersection of AI and game tooling at Hartmann Metaverse Ventures. He sourced Cathedral Studios (2022), a horror shooter with 300,000+ wishlists that became a portfolio standout, and led investment in Ludus AI, an Unreal Engine Co-Pilot to democratize game development. Daniel's sourced investments include Shapes XR, Cognitive 3D, Ludus AI, Neurable, Sloyd, Zibra AI, and Cathedral Studios. Outside work, he explores AI tools and experiments with Unreal Engine and Unity development.
