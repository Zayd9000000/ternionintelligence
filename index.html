A Complex World

In today's world, different interests compete for influence amid massive global challenges. The ideas and values that drive leaders, institutions, governments and movements remain largely hidden. What we see on the surface is often confusing - it's hard to tell propaganda and posturing from the real intentions and principles that will actually shape political and economic life. For organizations making investments, key decisions, or conducting diplomacy, this opacity creates both major risks and opportunities. Effective geopolitical intelligence can mean the difference between strategic success and costly miscalculation.

Our Solution

Ternion provides AI-powered strategic intelligence services that transform real-time data from human and digital sources into actionable insights, risk analysis, and strategic reports for organisations. Our coverage spans four continents, giving clients clarity regardless of context. We cut through the noise of surface-level events to identify the underlying forces that truly drive geopolitical change. This is enabled by an AI processing layer, that applies our proprietary ontology at scale.

Our Framework

The teleotypical framework is our proprietary ontology that categorises how power is exerted by different actors. It's built into our AI models as a processing layer that analyses all incoming data - from social media and public opinion research to qualitative interviews and official documents. This ontology allows us to process large-scale unstructured data and deliver clear insights about power dynamics to our clients.
